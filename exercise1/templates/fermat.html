Fermat's Little Theorem:

If p is a prime number and a is an integer not divisible by p, then:

a^(p-1) ≡ 1 (mod p)

  • Primality Test: If a^(n-1) ≢ 1 mod n for some a, then n is composite
  • Theorem Demo: Verify the theorem for specific a and p
  • Note: Fermat's test can give false positives for Carmichael numbers
